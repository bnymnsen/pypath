pypath.utils.mapping§

Performs mapping between IDs of different consensus systems for proteins and genes, miRNAs, and chemical compounds.

members:

Classes for reading and use serving ID mapping data from custom file, function, UniProt, UniProt uploadlists, Ensembl BioMart, PRO (Protein Ontology), miRBase or pickle file.
